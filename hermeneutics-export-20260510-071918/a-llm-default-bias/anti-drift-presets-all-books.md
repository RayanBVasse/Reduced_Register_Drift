Three layers of author-installed defenses **before the companion 
goes live**, captured per book:

1. **Intake notes** (pre-LLM) — the author's free-text directive to 
   the platform, written before Agent-1 has touched the manuscript.
2. **Tone + grounding + red-line** (Step-3) — bounded lever choices 
   plus an optional red-line text note.
3. **Avoid list** (Step-3) — the explicit, selected items the 
   author has ticked as "do not do this" — the paper's headline 
   evidence of anticipated commercial-LLM drift patterns.

Books are listed in the order they were onboarded so the reader can 
see the protocol maturing in response to earlier drift.
